The Challenge & Approach

The client needed to track air quality across 500 remote sites but existing solutions were too expensive. Our approach involved designing a custom low-power PCB using an ESP32 for connectivity.

We leveraged MQTT for efficient data transfer and built a serverless backend to handle millions of transactions per day, ensuring scalability from day one.

Results & Tech Stack

  • **Results:** 60% reduction in operational costs. Deployed in 3 months.
  • **Tech Stack:** ESP32, FreeRTOS, AWS IoT, React/Next.js, KiCad.
  • **Categories:** Embedded, IoT, Web
